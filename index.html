Thumbs – The Reflex Game!

A sneaky game of nerves, timing... and thumbs.

App Store (iOS) Google Play (Android)

Thumbs is a social reflex game where players compete to react quickly — and hold their thumb! Invite friends or join random games, and see who blinks first.

Features

  • Play with friends or random players
  • "Master of Thumb" mode
  • Randomized start timers for extra tension
  • Light-hearted animations and rewards
