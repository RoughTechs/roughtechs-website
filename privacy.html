Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 24, 2025

Thumbs ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect or share your personal information beyond what's necessary for gameplay and notifications.

What We Collect

How We Use It

Request Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and associated data from Thumbs, please send a request to developer@roughtechs.com using the email address linked to your account.

We will process your request within 7 days.
