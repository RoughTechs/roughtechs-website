Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 12, 2025

Roughtechs (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application Thumbs – The Reflex Game and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username/display name.
  • Identifiers: User ID (internal), device identifiers, and push notification tokens (for game updates).
  • Usage Data: App interactions, ad interaction data, and other usage data related to gameplay and features.
  • Diagnostics: Crash data, performance data, and other diagnostic information to improve stability and performance.
  • Location: We do not collect precise GPS location. Ad networks may infer general location (e.g., city/region) from your IP address to show relevant ads.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate core features (multiplayer gameplay, friends, invites, notifications).
  • Improve functionality, performance, and user experience.
  • Display ads and measure their performance (via Google AdMob).
  • Analyze usage trends and engagement (via Google Firebase Analytics).
  • Identify and fix crashes or bugs (via Firebase Crashlytics).

Advertising & Tracking

We use Google AdMob to show ads in the app. With personalized ads enabled, AdMob may collect device identifiers, usage data, and other signals to provide personalized advertising and measure effectiveness. This may involve tracking activity across other companies’ apps and websites.

On iOS, we request permission before enabling tracking under Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You can change your consent any time in your device settings.

Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and to improve it. Analytics data is used for our internal product development. Some analytics or usage signals may be shared with AdMob for advertising measurement.

Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash logs and performance data to diagnose and resolve issues. This data is not used for advertising.

Data Storage

Your account and game data are stored securely in Supabase (a cloud database service) using industry-standard security practices.

Your Choices & Controls

  • Personalized Ads: You can turn off personalized ads in your device’s ad settings.
  • Tracking (iOS): Manage permissions in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications in your device settings.
  • Access/Deletion: You can request account deletion or access to your data by contacting us or by using the in-app support feature.

Third‑Party Services

Our app uses the following third‑party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Policy
  • Firebase Privacy & Security
  • Supabase Privacy Policy

Request Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and associated data from Thumbs, please perform this action from within the App (In your Profile settings - choose "Delete Account") or by request deletion using the email address linked to your account. We will process your request within 7 days.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact support.
